Printed artwork on custom T-shirts may fade over time, especially after repeated washes, but the longevity of the design depends on several factors such as the printing method, high-quality inks, fabric type, and proper garment care. At Tee Print London, we specialize in high-quality custom T-shirt printing using durable inks and premium fabrics to ensure your designs stay vibrant longer.
